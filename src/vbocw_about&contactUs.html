About
Okay so this is one of those 2am ideas that keeps you awake all night. I(founder) got inspiration from Aaron Swartz, the internet's own boy. However I can't end up everything(what happened to Aaron is really sad and unfortunate). We are a group of 4 grad students form Dept. of Physics, VBU. We can not afford a server (or even a domain due to lack of funds), so everything is hosted on GitHub. We are working to improve this site in every possible way in our free time.
  • What do we do?
    • We collect useful notes/study materials and share them with everyone.
  • Where do we get those notes/materials from?
    • Usually they are contributed by student community. It could be someone's own handwritten/typed notes or any material available on the public domain.
  • Is everything free?
    • Each and every material is and will be free of cost.
  • I want to share my notes. How to do that?
    • Goto Contribute An Upload and fill the form. After reviewing it we will share it for you on VB OCW.
